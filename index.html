About Me

I will be moving to the Carnegie Mellon University ECE Department for my Ph.D. studies.

Hi, I am Oguzhan Karaahmetoglu, and I am a Data Scientist. Currently, I pursue my graduate studies at Bilkent University under the supervision of Prof. S. Serdar Kozat (http://www.ee.bilkent.edu.tr/~kozat/). I am a graduate of the Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department of Middle East Technical University (METU) with a GPA of 3.80/4.00. Moreover, I have been ranked 23rd among 2 Million students in the National University Entrance Exam.

I am currently part of an R&D team developing a spatio-temporal prediction framework. I work as a Machine Learning Expert at Databoss Inc (http://data-boss.com.tr//), where we have developed solutions for spatio-temporal prediction applications. Our framework is capable of processing various data types ranging from weather data to terrain data. Furthermore, we have specific approaches tailored for non-uniformly sampled and sparse time-series data.

My research interests include interpretability in machine learning and deep learning, spatio-temporal prediction, online learning, point process modeling, adaptive filtering, and big data.
